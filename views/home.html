Feast of Our Lady of Lourdes

  • Friday, Feb 11 is the Feast of Our Lady of Lourdes which Pope Francis has also declared World Day of Prayer for the Sick. In the Mass at 10am the sick in body and soul and seniors and those with mental illness may receive the Anointing of the Sick. Prayers will be offered for all the sick, those suffering from natural disasters anywhere, but especially in Tonga and the Pacific Islands.
